Tasty Stromboli Recipe

Description

This stromboli recipe uses premade pizza dough. Kids and adults love this pizza creation. There is usually leftover sauce, so you serve it the next day over hot spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) package pizza crust dough
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (14 ounce) jar pizza sauce
  • 1 cup cooked ham, diced
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced pepperoni sausage
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
