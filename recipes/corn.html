Irresistible Italian Corn

Description

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish. DELICIOUS. Must TRY NOW!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 1/2 cups whole kernal sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted; stir corn.
  2. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Cooked seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
